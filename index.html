DISCOVER TYPHOON

Typhoon is a new, innovative engine for the game and simulation industry. It is specialized in the real-time simulation of large, open worlds and includes many technologies commonly seen in commercial engines. In particular, Typhoon offers state-of-the art solutions for the simulation and rendering of oceans. They have been used with success in a commercial game, a scuba diving simulator and an underwater robotic simulation.
