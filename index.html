DISCOVER TYPHOON

Typhoon is a game engine specialized in the rendering and simulation of natural scenes of planetary scale. It features AAA quality technologies for the rendering of outer space, the Earth atmosphere, procedural terrains, oceans and underwater scenes.

Typhoon is written in C++ 17, Lua and HLSL and currently runs on the Windows / DirectX 11 platform.

It is the result of years of research and develoment and has been created by people with years of experience in the game industry, a deep understanding of the requirements of commercial engines and a love of the sea.

In 2020, the engine has been released as open source. Go to github to get the source code, report bugs and collaborate on new features.
